What is BruinList?
BruinList is a network of discussion forums for UCLA students. You can find events, people to carpool with, and sell or barter stuff. It's like Reddit or Craigslist, except its tailored to college topics.
How can I create an account?
Click on the "New User? Sign Up" button on the homepage and follow the registration process.
